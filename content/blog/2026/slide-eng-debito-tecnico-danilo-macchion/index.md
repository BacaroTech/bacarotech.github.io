**Technical debt** is one of the biggest challenges for any development team. In this talk **Danilo Macchion** tackles the topic head-on: what technical debt is, how it accumulates, how to recognize it and — most importantly — how to progressively manage and reduce it without stopping product development.

These slides are available in English.
